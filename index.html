Founder Member


MILESTONES INSTITUTE OF PHARMACEUTICAL SCIENCES

Tripura is established in 1st September 2022 and started functioning from 16th January 2023 after receiving all the Approvals & Affiliation from different- different authorities. It is run by Sanjeevamma Sure Chalapathi Foundation, Guwahati. The College aims to provide quality education in pharmaceutical sciences to meet the needs of students, pharmacists, pharmaceutical organizations and health care professionals.

About Institution


MILESTONES INSTITUTE OF PHARMACEUTICAL SCIENCES is approved by Health & Welfare Department, Govt. of Tripura & Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi (A statutory body under Ministry of Health & family Welfare Department, Govt. of India) & Affiliated to Tripura University (A Central University) & Tripura Board of Pharmacy Education, Agartala, Tripura.
