Aproval & Affiliation

MILESTONES INSTITUTE OF PHARMACEUTICAL SCIENCES is approved by Health & Welfare Department, Govt. of Tripura & Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi (A statutory body under Ministry of Health & family Welfare Department, Govt. of India) & Affiliated to Tripura University (A Central University) & Tripura Board of Pharmacy Education, Agartala, Tripura.

Vision

  • To become a global institute imparting quality pharmaceutical education, research and practice with the ultimate goal of providing excellent platforms to create leaders and inventors and generate new knowledge for society and industry.

  • To transform the institute into global center of learning through application of creativity, innovation and discipline.

  • The faculty and students of the MIPS will be innovators and leaders in transforming health care to create positive patient outcomes through the discovery and translation of research and scholarship. The quality and completeness of our students’ education is our top priority.

    1. We pursue excellence in education and scholarship.
    2. We have the responsibility to assure that our graduates will safeguard the health of the public.
    3. There is community within the faculty and staff that is premised on collegiality, mutual trust, respect, honesty, and integrity.
    4. We value diversity within our faculty, staff, students, teaching, and scholarship in helping us to be better members of our community and society.
    5. The College is responsible to its students, staff, and faculty to help them grow personally and professionally.

Mission

  • Our mission is to advance societal health through leadership in pharmacy education, research, community engagement, and improved patient care.

  • Create ideal ambience for learning and growth.

  • Help students inherit value systems, be creative and agile thinkers.

  • Establish Discipline, Value Added Education and Training & Placement as Three Core value.

  • Building capabilities among students to lead from the front as also be a team player.

  • Develop a symbiotic relationship between the institution, society, and the community, for mutual betterment.

  • Expand the vistas of higher learning in Technology and Management Fields, including Post Graduate and research.

  • Encourage Global Vision and integration with International best practices for local, regional and national development.

Objective

  • To Provide infrastructure and research facilities to disseminate the advanced knowledge to the students in various branches pharmaceutical sciences through innovative teaching-learning process with inter-disciplinary approach such that they grow their wisdom to acquire all the knowledge and generate new ideas.

  • To educate and train manpower for the development of the country and establish linkages with industries for the promotion of sciences and technology.

  • To develop the spirit of internationalism and competitiveness in students such that they develop new original ideas and make new discoveries and inventions to make a strong society

  • To encourage students for, acquiring self-confidence and instill moral values in students making them well disciplined.

  • To pay special attention to the improvement of the social and economic conditions and welfare of the people from the health point of view.
