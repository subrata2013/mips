Pharmacy Career

Today the discipline of pharmacy has made enormous progress and has matured as distinctly independent branch as Pharmaceutical Science, mainly through the acquisition of wealth of knowledge, research and a vast array of drugs and therapeutic remedies.

Scope After D. Pharm –


  1. Production and manufacturing & Pharmaceutical packaging.

    2. After completion of D. Pharma a pharmacy professional can work as a production person, involved in the production of bulk drug or intermediate and formulations and dosage forms and also work in the cosmetic industries for the preparation and packaging of various cosmetic products.

  2. Hospital Pharmacist

    3. Another opening for a pharmacy professional is a registered pharmacist in the hospitals and drug stores.

  3. Entrepreneurship

    4. An aspirant of a highly bright future can enter through various openings like starting an own retail or wholesale drug store or become a professional entrepreneur.

  4. Marketing

    5. The pharma sales & marketing is a highly technical field & offers excellent opportunities for pharmacy professionals.

  5. Higher studies

    6. After completion of D. Pharma students can go for B Pharma as a lateral entry on third semester.

  6. Government Jobs

    7. A number of opportunities are waiting after completing D. Pharm in government sectors as community pharmacist, hospital pharmacist, drug stores management etc.

Scope After B. Pharm –


  1. Pharma Industry

    2. B. Pharma professional have wide scope on pharma marketing and offers excellent opportunity to build his/her carrier as a marketing professional.

    After completion of B. Pharm he/she can start carrier in production units of Pharma Industries as a QA/ QC Chemist and pharma production.

  2. Clinical Pharmacist

    3. After completion of B. Pharm a wide opportunities is there in clinical trials.

  3. Government jobs

    4. Several government job opportunities are waiting for B. Pharm professionals i.e. Drug inspector, Hospital pharmacist, Railway pharmacist, National Health Mission Pharmacist.

  4. Entrepreneurship

    5. An aspirant of a highly bright future can enter through various openings like starting an own retail or wholesale drug store or become a professional entrepreneur.

  5. Academics

    6. After completing B. Pharm one can work as a lecturer in any D. Pharmacy College.
